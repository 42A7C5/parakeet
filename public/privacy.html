Information We Collect

When you create an account on Parakeet, we may collect your email address, Parakeet name, and profile picture. No other information is provided to us by Google or any other login provider.

During your gameplay sessions on Parakeet, we may collect basic gameplay information, such as your scores, game progress, and achievements. This information is collected by the developer of the game, and may be stored on Parakeet servers. Please note that some games do not use the Parakeet services, and data collected may be used according to that game’s privacy policy.

Parakeet may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience and collect information about how you interact with our website. This may include information about your device, browser type, IP address, and pages visited.

How We Use Collected Information

We use the information collected to personalize your gaming experience, including displaying your achievements and progress, providing recommendations, and syncing your name and profile across games.

The data we collect allows us to analyze user behavior and preferences, helping us to improve our website, games, and services.

In some cases, we may need to use your information to comply with legal obligations or to protect our rights and interests.

How We Protect Your Information

Parakeet is committed to safeguarding your data and maintaining its confidentiality. We implement appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information, username, password, transaction information, and data stored on our website.

We may share your gameplay information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you. These service providers are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of your information. Short version: we don’t sell your data, and we NEVER share your personal information, except with Google and Google’s Firebase for the sole purpose of facilitating login.

We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information with our partners, advertisers, or other third parties for marketing, analytics, or other purposes.

We may disclose your information in response to a lawful request by public authorities, court orders, or as required by law to protect our rights or the safety of our users.
